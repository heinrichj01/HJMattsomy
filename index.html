Who we are?

# Our Strategy

Mattsomy21 is a dedicated initiative focused on raising awareness about Down Syndrome among children in South Africa. We achieve this through storytelling, research, and advocacy, with the goal of co-creating new narratives for children with Down Syndrome.

# Our Aim

We strive to comprehend the context of Down Syndrome in South Africa by launching action research initiatives aimed at addressing critical systemic issues that impact the enhancement and well-being outcomes for children with Down Syndrome.

Research

# Addressing Data Gaps for Children with Down Syndrome in South Africa

There is a notable lack of specific data concerning children with Down Syndrome in the South African context. Our goal is to ensure that our research initiatives are accessible and inclusive, providing valuable insights that can shape programming and policy. Through a collaborative, multi-sectoral approach, we want to connect science with everyday people and create growing narratives for case management of Down Syndrome in South Africa.
